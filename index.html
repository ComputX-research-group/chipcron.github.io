About Us

Our company is a technology-driven organization that specializes in developing innovative and game-changing solutions for the education and technology industries. We are proud to offer our cutting-edge product that are designed to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our product, Customcore, is an innovative platform for designing and optimizing custom System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions. We understand that every client has unique requirements, and that's why we offer a fully customizable SoC that is tailored to the Indian IoT market. Our bespoke processor and built-in self-test capabilities make our platform the perfect choice for clients looking to empower local innovation. We take great pride in our indigenous design and believe that it is our attention to detail that sets us apart.

We are also working on designing upcoming future chips introducing Built-in-self-repair and embedded FPGAs to expand the horizon of computing. We are also focusing on changing the paradigm of computing for post sensor read processing near data stream itself.

At our company, we are driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence. We are constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible and are always striving to create solutions that will make a meaningful impact on our clients' businesses. Our team is made up of the brightest minds, and we work tirelessly to ensure that our products are of the highest quality.

Thank you for considering our company, and we look forward to working with you to create innovative solutions that will help you achieve your goals.
